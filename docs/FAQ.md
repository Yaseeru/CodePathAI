# CodePath AI - Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Last Updated:** January 2025

## General Questions

### What is CodePath AI?

CodePath AI is a free, AI-powered coding education platform that provides personalized learning experiences. Unlike traditional coding courses that start with "What language do you want to learn?", we start with "What do you want to build?" and create a custom roadmap to help you achieve your specific goals.

---

### Is CodePath AI really free?

Yes! CodePath AI is completely free to use. No credit card required, no hidden fees, no trial periods. We believe everyone should have access to quality coding education.

**What's included for free:**
- Unlimited lessons
- AI mentor access
- Project building
- Progress tracking
- All platform features

---

### Who is CodePath AI for?

CodePath AI is designed for:

- **Career Transitioners** - Professionals looking to switch to tech
- **Busy Learners** - People with limited time (15-minute sessions)
- **App Builders** - Non-technical founders who want to prototype
- **Curious Students** - Anyone interested in learning to code
- **Goal-Oriented Learners** - People who want to build something specific

**You don't need any prior coding experience!**

---

### How is CodePath AI different from other coding platforms?

**Key differences:**

1. **Goal-First Approach** - Start with what you want to build, not what language to learn
2. **AI Personalization** - Every interaction is tailored to your progress and goals
3. **Micro-Sessions** - 15-minute focused lessons that fit any schedule
4. **Build-as-You-Learn** - Real projects aligned with your goals, not abstract exercises
5. **Context-Aware AI Mentor** - Remembers your progress and adapts to your needs

## Learning Experience

### How long are the lessons?

Each lesson is designed to be completed in **15 minutes or less**. This makes it easy to:

- Learn during lunch breaks
- Fit learning into busy schedules
- Maintain focus and retention
- Build consistent habits

**You can take longer if needed** - 15 minutes is a guide, not a limit.

---

### What if I need more than 15 minutes for a lesson?

That's completely fine! The 15-minute target is a guideline, not a strict rule. You can:

- Pause and resume anytime
- Take breaks between sections
- Review material multiple times
- Take as long as you need to understand

**Learning at your own pace is encouraged!**

---

### Can I go faster and complete multiple lessons per day?

Absolutely! You can:

- Complete as many lessons as you want
- Work through your roadmap at your own pace
- Spend more time on weekends
- Accelerate your learning

**Just remember:** Understanding is more important than speed.

## AI Mentor

### How does the AI Mentor work?

The AI Mentor is powered by Claude (Anthropic's AI) and:

- Remembers your progress and goals
- Adapts explanations to your experience level
- Provides personalized guidance
- Available 24/7

**It's like having a personal coding tutor** that knows your learning journey.

---

### What can I ask the AI Mentor?

You can ask anything related to coding and your lessons:

**Good questions:**
- "How do I declare a variable?"
- "Why isn't my code working?"
- "Can you explain this concept differently?"
- "What's the best way to solve this problem?"
- "I'm stuck on this exercise, can you give me a hint?"

**The AI Mentor won't:**
- Give you complete answers (you need to learn!)
- Do your homework
- Write entire projects for you

**It will:**
- Guide you toward solutions
- Explain concepts
- Provide hints and tips
- Help you debug

---

### How fast does the AI Mentor respond?

**Very fast!** Typically:

- First response: < 2 seconds
- Complete response: < 5 seconds
- Streaming responses for real-time feedback

---

### Can the AI Mentor help with projects?

Yes! The AI Mentor can:

- Help you understand project requirements
- Provide guidance on approach
- Help debug your code
- Explain concepts you're struggling with
- Give hints without spoiling the solution

## Troubleshooting

### I forgot my password. What do I do?

1. Go to login page
2. Click "Forgot Password?"
3. Enter your email
4. Check email for reset link
5. Create new password

**Reset link expires in 24 hours.**

---

### My code isn't running. What's wrong?

**Common issues:**

1. **Syntax errors** - Check for typos, missing brackets, semicolons
2. **Timeout** - Code takes too long (30 second limit)
3. **Network issues** - Check your internet connection
4. **Browser issues** - Try refreshing the page

**Still stuck?** Ask the AI Mentor for help!

---

### The AI Mentor isn't responding. What should I do?

**Try these steps:**

1. Refresh the page
2. Check your internet connection
3. Try again in a few seconds
4. Clear browser cache

**If problem persists:** Contact support@codepath-ai.com

---

### I found a bug. How do I report it?

**We appreciate bug reports!**

**Email:** support@codepath-ai.com

**Include:**
- Description of the bug
- Steps to reproduce
- Browser and device info
- Screenshots (if applicable)

---

### The page is loading slowly. Why?

**Possible reasons:**

1. Slow internet connection
2. Browser cache needs clearing
3. Too many browser tabs open
4. Server maintenance (rare)

**Try:**
- Refresh the page
- Clear browser cache
- Close other tabs
- Check internet speed
