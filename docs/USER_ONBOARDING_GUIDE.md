# CodePath AI - User Onboarding Guide

**Welcome to CodePath AI!** 🚀

Your personal AI-powered coding mentor that helps you learn to code by building what you want to build.

---

## What is CodePath AI?

CodePath AI is a free, personalized coding education platform that:

- 🎯 **Starts with YOUR goals** - Tell us what you want to build, not what language you want to learn
- 🤖 **Provides AI mentorship** - Get help from an AI mentor that remembers your progress and adapts to your needs
- ⏱️ **Fits your schedule** - Learn in focused 15-minute micro-sessions
- 🛠️ **Builds real projects** - Create actual applications aligned with your goals
- 📈 **Tracks your progress** - See your growth with detailed progress tracking

---

## Getting Started (5 minutes)

### Step 1: Create Your Account

1. Go to [codepath-ai.com](https://codepath-ai.com)
2. Click "Get Started" or "Sign Up"
3. Enter your name, email, and create a password
4. Click "Create Account"

**That's it!** No credit card required. CodePath AI is free to use.

---

### Step 2: Tell Us Your Goal

The magic of CodePath AI starts with understanding what YOU want to build.

**What to enter:**
- Be specific about what you want to create
- Don't worry about technical details
- Focus on the outcome you want

**Good examples:**
- ✅ "Build a task management app for my team"
- ✅ "Create a personal portfolio website to showcase my work"
- ✅ "Develop a weather dashboard with real-time data"
- ✅ "Build a chat application for my community"

**Not-so-good examples:**
- ❌ "Learn JavaScript" (too vague)
- ❌ "Become a developer" (not specific enough)
- ❌ "Code" (what do you want to build?)

**Pro tip:** If you're not sure, click "Show me examples" for inspiration!

---

### Step 3: Set Your Schedule

Tell us how much time you can dedicate to learning each week.

**Options:**
- **2-3 hours/week** - Perfect for busy schedules (2-3 micro-sessions per week)
- **5-7 hours/week** - Balanced learning (daily 15-min sessions)
- **10+ hours/week** - Intensive learning (multiple sessions daily)

**Remember:** CodePath AI is designed for 15-minute sessions, so even 2-3 hours/week means consistent progress!

---

### Step 4: Share Your Experience

Let us know your current coding experience so we can tailor the content to your level.

**Options:**
- **Complete Beginner** - Never written code before
- **Some Experience** - Tried coding tutorials or courses
- **Intermediate** - Built small projects, comfortable with basics
- **Advanced** - Experienced developer learning new skills

**Don't worry:** There's no wrong answer! We'll adapt as you learn.

---

### Step 5: Get Your Personalized Roadmap

Click "Generate My Roadmap" and watch the magic happen!

**What's happening:**
- Our AI analyzes your goal, schedule, and experience
- Creates a custom learning path just for you
- Selects lessons and projects aligned with your goal
- Estimates time for each lesson (15-minute sessions)

**This takes about 10-20 seconds.** Grab a coffee! ☕

---

### Step 6: Start Learning!

Your personalized roadmap is ready! Here's what you'll see:

**Roadmap View:**
- 📚 **Lessons** - Ordered by prerequisites, each ~15 minutes
- 🚀 **Projects** - Real applications you'll build
- 📊 **Progress** - Track your completion percentage
- ⏱️ **Time Estimates** - Know what to expect

**Click "Start Learning"** to begin your first lesson!

## Key Features

### 🤖 AI Mentor

Your personal coding assistant that:
- Remembers your progress and goals
- Adapts explanations to your level
- Provides hints without giving away answers
- Available 24/7

**How to use:**
1. Click "Ask AI Mentor" button
2. Type your question in plain English
3. Get a personalized response
4. Ask follow-up questions

**Example questions:**
- "How do I declare a variable in JavaScript?"
- "Why isn't my code working?"
- "Can you explain this concept differently?"
- "What's the best way to approach this problem?"

---

### 💻 Code Editor

A professional code editor built into the platform:

**Features:**
- Syntax highlighting
- Auto-completion
- Error detection
- Line numbers
- Auto-save (every 30 seconds)

**Keyboard Shortcuts:**
- `Ctrl + S` (or `Cmd + S`) - Save code
- `Ctrl + Enter` (or `Cmd + Enter`) - Run code
- `Ctrl + Z` (or `Cmd + Z`) - Undo
- `Ctrl + Y` (or `Cmd + Y`) - Redo

**Running Code:**
1. Write your code in the editor
2. Click "Run Code" button (or use `Ctrl + Enter`)
3. See output in the console below
4. Fix any errors and run again

## Tips for Success

### 1. Set a Schedule

**Best practice:** Pick a specific time each day for your 15-minute session.

**Examples:**
- Morning coffee + coding
- Lunch break learning
- Evening wind-down with code

**Why it works:** Consistency builds habits.

---

### 2. Start Small

**Don't try to do everything at once!**

- Complete one lesson at a time
- Focus on understanding, not speed
- It's okay to take breaks
- Review previous lessons if needed

---

### 3. Use the AI Mentor

**Don't struggle alone!**

- Ask questions when stuck
- Request different explanations
- Get hints for exercises
- Discuss concepts

**Remember:** There are no "dumb questions" - the AI is here to help!

---

### 4. Practice Regularly

**15 minutes daily > 2 hours once a week**

- Short, frequent sessions are more effective
- Your brain needs time to process
- Consistency builds skills faster
- Maintain your streak!

---

### 5. Build Projects

**Projects solidify your learning:**

- Apply what you've learned
- Create something tangible
- Build your portfolio
- Gain confidence

**Don't skip projects!** They're where real learning happens.

---

### 6. Track Your Progress

**Celebrate your wins:**

- Check your dashboard regularly
- See how far you've come
- Unlock achievements
- Share your progress

**Motivation matters!** Seeing progress keeps you going.

## Common Questions

### How long does it take to complete the roadmap?

It depends on your time commitment and pace:

- **2-3 hours/week:** 3-6 months
- **5-7 hours/week:** 2-3 months
- **10+ hours/week:** 1-2 months

**Remember:** Everyone learns at their own pace. Focus on understanding, not speed.

---

### Can I change my goal?

**Yes!** You can pivot your goal anytime:

1. Go to your Progress Dashboard
2. Click "Change Goal"
3. Enter your new goal
4. Get a new personalized roadmap

**Your completed lessons and projects are preserved** in your learning history.

---

### What if I get stuck?

**You have multiple options:**

1. **Ask the AI Mentor** - Get instant help
2. **Review previous lessons** - Refresh your memory
3. **Take a break** - Come back with fresh eyes
4. **Try a different approach** - Experiment!

**Getting stuck is part of learning!** Don't give up.

---

### Can I skip lessons?

**Lessons are ordered by prerequisites**, so we recommend following the sequence. However:

- You can review any completed lesson
- You can pause and resume anytime
- You can take breaks between lessons

**Trust the roadmap** - it's designed for your success.

---

- ✅ Projects in your portfolio
- ✅ Streak days accumulate
- ✅ Achievements unlock
- ✅ Concepts become clearer

**If you're showing up and learning, you're making progress!**
